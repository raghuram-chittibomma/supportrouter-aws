# Returns Policy

VoltEdge Electronics accepts returns of eligible items within **30 days** of delivery when products are unused and in original packaging.

Damaged or missing shipment claims should include order ID (format `VE-####`) and photos when applicable.

Refunds at or below **$100 USD** may be issued automatically by the support agent after eligibility checks. Refunds **above $100 USD** require supervisor approval.
