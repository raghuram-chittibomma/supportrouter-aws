# PowerDock Duo Compatibility

The PowerDock Duo USB-C dock supports DisplayPort Alt Mode over USB-C. It is compatible with laptops that provide video over USB-C.

It is **not** guaranteed to charge devices that require proprietary USB-C power delivery profiles beyond USB-C PD 100W.
